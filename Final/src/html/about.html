About our Company Our Goal

We are not your run of the mill type of kennel, we are small facility in a scenic and calm country setting. We are here to provide a special service to your family member. The services we currently provide are Boarding and Daycare. We are currently working toward a Dog Park and Grooming Services in the future. Our long term goal is to become a trusted and well respected dog kennel in our community.

We guarantee that you and your pets will fall in love with our home that will be your pets new home away from home!
