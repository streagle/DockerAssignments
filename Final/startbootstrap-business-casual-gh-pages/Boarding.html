Boarding What you should bring

Boarding What We Provide

  • Indoor runs 6x6 with heated floors for winter and air condition for summer
  • Outdoor runs 6x8 with walls separating dogs for privacy and non-cross contamination
  • Extra large run area for outside playtime individually (always supervised)
  • Stress free potty zone in certain kennel areas
  • 5 Large exercise areas
